# Dungeon-Crawler-Game

<p align="center">
  <img src="img/banner.png" width="65%">
</p>

A platformer game paying homage to the MS-DOS games of the 90s , made on the Godot Engine using GDScript as an educational project. Players must navigate hazardous levels, collect valuable items, defeat enemies, and reach the exit while managing health, lives, and equipment.

---

## 🎮 Features

- Ten handcrafted platforming levels
- Shooting mechanics with bidirectional bullets
- Collectible gun, gems and key system
- Jetpack with limited fuel
- Enemy AI with bidirectional projectile attacks
- Health and lives system
- Animated pickups and hazards
- HUD displaying:
  - Score
  - Lives
  - Health
  - Key status
  - Gun status
  - Jetpack fuel bar

## 🎯 Gameplay

Explore ten increasingly challenging levels while avoiding environmental hazards and hostile enemies.

Collect useful items along the way:

- 💎 Gems to increase your score
- 🔑 Key to unlock the exit
- 🔫 Gun to defeat enemies
- 🚀 Jetpack to access difficult areas

Reach the exit door to advance to the next level. Complete all ten levels to finish the game.
